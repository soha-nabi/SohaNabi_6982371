Community News

Catch up on our latest local stories, ecological achievements, and announcements.

Green City Initiative

Our monthly volunteer cleanup at Central Park brought together over 120 dedicated community members this weekend. Families, students, and local businesses collaborated to plant over fifty native maple saplings, repaint aged picnic tables, and collect recyclable waste from the trail paths. Organized by the Eco-Action Club, this marks our single largest environmental turnout this year.

Empowering Youth in Tech

In cooperation with the Innovation Hub, we hosted a free frontend development bootcamp this Saturday. Twenty-five enthusiastic teenagers got hands-on experience building their very first responsive websites using semantic HTML and CSS Grid layout concepts. Due to overwhelming interest, we are preparing an advanced CSS3 and Javascript series starting next month.

Art Walk Returns!

Mark your calendars! The annual Community Craft and Art Fair returns on June 15th at Community Hall A. This year, we're proud to feature forty-five local painters, ceramicists, sculptors, and woodworkers. Interactive workshops, street performances, food trucks, and kids' activities will run all afternoon, ensuring a fun day out for families.
